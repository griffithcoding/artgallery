Since 2015 · Williamsburg, Brooklyn

A gallery that keeps its archive open.

VERSO is an independent contemporary art gallery in Brooklyn. We represent emerging and mid-career artists, mount a new exhibition roughly every six weeks, and — unusually — put our entire inventory of more than 20,000 works online, each one with its price.

What we believe

Transparency is the program.

For most of the art world’s history, the price of a work has been the last thing you’re told. We think that’s backwards. Sixty-nine percent of collectors say they’ve walked away from a purchase because they couldn’t find the price — so we publish it, on every available work, from a few hundred dollars to the top of our program.

That openness shapes everything: a website built like a public archive, viewing rooms that edit it down to a single idea, and a front desk that answers questions in plain language. The art is ambitious. The buying shouldn’t be intimidating.
