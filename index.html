The gallery

A curated program, shown in the open.

We opened VERSO in 2015 on a simple premise: a great gallery shouldn’t hide most of its program in a back room. So we show the whole program in the open — every available work on its own page with a clear status — and let the exhibitions do the editing. The result is a gallery you can walk through in person on Wythe Avenue, or browse from anywhere.
